Lived Experience Meets Strategic Expertise

As an educator, consultant, and strategist, I work with organizations that are serious about creating meaningful, sustainable change - not just checking boxes.

My approach bridges academic rigor with lived experience, helping leadership teams design training, programs, and strategies that are evidence-based, culturally responsive, and defensible to boards, funders, staff, and communities.

I am committed to empowering organizations to create meaningful, sustainable change through education, strategy, and partnership.
